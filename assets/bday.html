SKINCARE ROUTINE

Creating a comprehensive skincare routine involves layering the right products at the right time. Here's a daily routine that incorporates your mentioned products:

    ### Morning Routine:
  1. **Cleanser**: Use a gentle cleanser to remove any impurities from the night.
  2. **Toner**: Apply a hydrating toner to prepare your skin for the following products.
  3. **Niacinamide Serum**: Helps with pigmentation and regulates oil production.
  4. **Vitamin C Serum**: Apply a few drops to help with brightness and overall skin tone.
  5. **Moisturizer**: Use a lightweight, non-comedogenic moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated.
  6. **Sunscreen**: Apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen (SPF 30 or higher) as the final step to protect your skin from UV damage.
    ### Evening Routine:
  1. **Cleanser**: Use a gentle cleanser to remove makeup, sunscreen, and impurities from the day.
  2. **Toner**: Apply a hydrating toner to prepare your skin.
  3. **Azelaic Acid**: Apply this to help reduce acne redness and inflammation.
  4. **Tranexamic Acid Serum**: Use this to address pigmentation issues.
  5. **Moisturizer**: Apply a nourishing moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated overnight.
### Twice a Week: - **Chemical Peeling Solution (32%)**: Use this in the evening, but not on the same nights as your azelaic acid or tranexamic acid to avoid irritation. Apply it to clean, dry skin, leave it on for the recommended time, then rinse off thoroughly. Follow with a hydrating moisturizer. ### Additional Tips: - **Patch Test**: Before introducing any new product, do a patch test to ensure your skin doesn't react negatively. - **Introduce Products Gradually**: Start with one active ingredient and gradually introduce others to avoid overwhelming your skin. - **Hydration and Diet**: Drink plenty of water and maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables to support your skin health from within. - **Consistency**: Stick to your routine consistently to see the best results over time. Feel free to adjust this routine based on your skin's response and tolerance to each product.
